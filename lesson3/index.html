Davis County Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

(1) Grow membership, (2) facilitate networking, (3) enable engagement, and (4) educate and empower action.

Grow Membership

Market the function and benefits of Chamber of Commerce membership. Convert prospective members into registered members.

Facilitate Networking

Provide contact information of existing members and communicate chamber hierarchy, roles, and responsibilities.

Enable Engagement

Advertise chamber and community events. Display the chamber calendar.

Educate and Empower Action

Provide educational resources for businesses and community members.
