Personas

Persona 1

Name: Mark Treu

Age: 44

Education: Associates Degree

Family Status: Married, Four Children

Role/Job: Managing Partner/Owner of Consulting Firm

Goal/Task Pursued on Website: Gather information to aid him in deciding if he should join the Chamber.

What Matters Most to Them: Having started and sold multiple businesses, Mark considers himself a self-made man. He values pragmatism and results over common convention. He's heard other local business owners say the Chamber has been a value-add for the business, but wants to check the facts out for himself. He's coming to the site to see what the Chamber says about itself, before having lunch with a few colleagues to talk about their experience as Chamber members.

Persona 2

Name: Tamara "Tammy" England

Age: 50

Education: Bachelor's Degree

Family Status: Married, Empty Nester

Role/Job: Owner/Operator of Two Local Boutiques

Goal/Task Pursued on Website: Gather information about the upcoming "Shop Local" event being hosted by the Chamber.

What Matters Most to Them: Tammy has been a Chamber member for 7 years. Though her life as a dual-business owner is hectic, she relishes the opportunity to own her own destiny, explore a creative outlet, and command the freedom to make time for her grandkids. After letting the last two "Shop Local" events slip by her, she's determined to get involved in this year's event to leverage it as an opportunity to network prospective partnerships and, hopefully, break the revenue ceiling they've been hitting the last two fiscal years. She wants to know which chamber member is heading up the event and when the weekly planning meetings are to see if she can fit it into her schedule.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

A member of the county who wants to know who leads the Chamber of Commerce.

Scenario 2

A business owner and prospective member wants to know what other businesses are members of the chamber.

Scenario 3

A business owner and prospective member wants to know about upcoming events for business networking.

Scenario 4

A member of the county wants to know the next Chamber of Commerce event they can attend.

Scenario 5

A business owner wants to know what online resources for businesses the chamber offers.
