Rationale

Montserrat

This font was chosen to optimize accessibility as well as to convey a sense of accessible professionalism.

This font maximizes accessibility because of its sans-serif type, which improves readability on screens. The font's style elevates the user's experience with an aire of professionalism, while still retaining a sense of accessibility. These qualities are desirable for a website of this purpose because it connects to the site's dual nature of working with businesses as well as local community members who need to feel like they belong there, too.

Cabin

This font was chosen to match the goals and tone of the primary font, without getting in its way.

The sans serif type font increases screen readability and the tone of the font matches the professionalism and accessbility of the primary font. In addition, the weight and kearning of the font make it easy to distinguish from the primary font while still honoring the primary's style.
